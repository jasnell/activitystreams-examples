Actions

Portable, Declarative Potential Action Descriptions

These things exist everywhere currently in the form of: Social Widgets, Cards, Actions in the Inbox, oEmbed, etc.

The challenge right now, however, is that there are no standards, no consistent implementation, and everything is controlled by specific individual vendors.

The Potential Actions Vocabulary models after schema.org/Actions without depending on schema.org/Actions.
